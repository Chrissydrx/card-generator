## Design Context

### Users
Friends playing a drinking game at a house party or bar. Casual setting, low stakes, good vibes. The cards are printed at home on a regular printer, cut with scissors.

### Brand Personality
Chill, friendly, playful. Not trying too hard, not edgy or aggressive. Think lazy Sunday afternoon with friends, not a nightclub.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference**: Classic card games like UNO -- clean, recognizable, simple shapes, bold flat colors
- **Anti-reference**: Neon-on-dark "gamer" aesthetics, gradient-heavy designs, glassmorphism
- **Theme**: Light card backgrounds (warm off-white), solid color accents per card type
- **No gradients**: Flat colors only
- **Printed medium**: Must look crisp at poker card size (63x88mm) on home printers

### Design Principles
1. **Flat and bold** -- Solid colors, no gradients, no shadows. Color blocks do the work.
2. **Readable first** -- Text must be legible at small printed size. Clear hierarchy.
3. **Two-color system** -- Warm coral for role cards, cool teal for rule cards. Consistent and recognizable.
4. **Less is more** -- No emojis, no ornaments, no decorative noise. Let typography and color carry the design.
